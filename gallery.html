Exploring Communism in Cuba Today

Communism has played a significant role in shaping the world's political landscape. The map above depicts the distribution of communist countries between 1979 and 1983. It represents a period of time when several nations embraced communist ideologies and implemented socialist systems.

The artwork showcases an intriguing metaphorical representation of communism as a transformative force. It portrays a caterpillar devouring the symbol of capitalism, emphasizing the revolutionary nature of communist ideals.

The video provides a glimpse into the current state of communism in Cuba. It is important to approach this topic with an open mind and acknowledge the complexities and diverse perspectives surrounding it.

By studying communism's historical context and contemporary manifestations, we can better understand its impact on societies and appreciate the various viewpoints surrounding this ideology.
